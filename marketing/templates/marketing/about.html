About Le Frat

Le Frat is more than a brand—it’s a way of being. Born from friendship, authenticity, and a shared love for doing things right, Le Frat stands for simplicity with character. What started around burgers became a space where people come together, connect, and feel part of something real.

Every piece we create reflects that same mindset. No excess, no pretence—just quality, intention, and attitude. Our merchandise extends the Le Frat experience beyond the table, letting the community carry the brand with them, wherever they go.

Simple, well made, and meant to be shared—just like friendship.
